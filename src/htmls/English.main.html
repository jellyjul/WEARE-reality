Differences between virtual reality

and augmented reality

Virtual reality

Virtual reality
Differs from augmented reality (AR) in that visible reality is artificial. The user is offered complete immersion, using additional senses. We implement the latest VR developments - we use not only helmet, as well as gloves, suits and motion controllers.

Augmented reality
Technology with which into visible reality place virtual objects. Imagine that you are on presentation of an architect, put on special glasses, and A model of a building appears on an empty table. With a movement of your hand you you can rotate an object, zoom in, out, or even something change it.
